Belief is described as a form of magic closely associated with the Field of the First, which can alter reality within certain limits. The strength of belief varies based on factors such as the source and number of believers, and it is especially potent in planes where belief shapes reality. However, belief cannot override fundamental metaphysical laws, and some truths exist outside of belief. This mechanic allows for phenomena such as wish magic, powerful gods shaped by faith, and belief-based paradoxes in higher planes. In terms of class affinities, there is no clear alignment with the Field of the First, but it may have influence on all classes. Divine magic is associated with the Field of Divine Magic, but the specific classes aligned with other Fields are not detailed in the notes.
