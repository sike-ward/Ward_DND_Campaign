# How Magic Works

- All magic is rooted in metaphysical Fields, each with a unique nature and influence.
- Most spellcasters draw magic indirectly—through rituals, material, emotional, spiritual, or planar conduits.
- Divine power comes from the will of planar entities or cosmic concepts.
- Psionics may stem from the Astral or Thought Planes.
- Each school and spell often draws from multiple Fields in complex ways.
