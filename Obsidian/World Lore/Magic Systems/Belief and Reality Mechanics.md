# Belief and Reality Mechanics

Belief itself is a form of magic—a force most closely tied to the enigmatic Field of the First. It alters reality, especially in conceptual planes, but with limits.

### Influence of Belief

- **Strength:** Varies based on the source, depth, clarity, and number of believers.
- **Location:** Stronger in planes where belief shapes reality (e.g., Outlands, Sigil).

### Limits

- Belief cannot override certain fundamental metaphysical laws.
- Some truths exist outside or beyond belief—possibly linked to the First or other Fields.
- Example: If everyone believed they didn’t exist, they would not simply vanish.

---

> _This mechanic allows for phenomena such as wish magic, powerful gods shaped by faith, or belief-based paradoxes in higher planes._
