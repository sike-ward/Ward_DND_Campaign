# Class Affinities by Field

A draft association of D&D classes and subclasses with each magical Field.

- **Field of the First:** No clear class alignment. Possibly influential in all.
- **Field of Divine Magic:** Cleric, Paladin, Druid
- **Field of Arcane Magic:** Wizard, Sorcerer, Artificer
- **Field of Nature Magic:** Druid, Ranger
- **Field of Psionic Magic:** Fighter, Rogue (psionic archetypes)
- **Field of Dunamancy:** None explicitly (may appear in subclasses only)
- **Field of Hemocraft:** None (homebrew only)
- **Field of Soul Magic:** None (thematic, but no core class)
- **Field of Wild Magic:** None (usually tied to fey/elemental sources)
- **Field of Far Magic:** Warlock

_Note: School and subclass associations may change as Fields are further developed._
