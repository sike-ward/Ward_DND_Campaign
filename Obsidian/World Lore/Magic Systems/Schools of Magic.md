# Schools of Magic

Most mortal spellcasters manipulate energy shaped by the Fields, filtered through their culture and training. Only Arcane Magic is fully structured into the classic eight “Schools”:

- **Abjuration**
- **Conjuration**
- **Divination**
- **Enchantment**
- **Evocation**
- **Illusion**
- **Necromancy**
- **Transmutation**

Other Fields sometimes manifest unique “schools” (e.g., Chronurgy and Graviturgy in Dunamancy), but these are rare and less formalized. Divine and Hemocraft magic operate on different metaphysical principles and do not use “schools.”

_Magic is theoretically infinite, but mortal access depends on will, belief, location, and conduit (rituals, faith, willpower, etc.)._
