# Magic System Overview

Magic in this world is rooted in the metaphysical structure of the multiverse. It flows from various “Fields”—foundational forces that shape magical expression and determine how mortals and immortals alike interact with reality.

- Magic originates from the Planes themselves, influenced by existence, emotion, and thought.
- Theories on the origin and nature of these Fields differ among scholars and practitioners.
- Access to magic is shaped by belief, location, will, and attunement to specific Fields.
